-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dataset structure
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SynHDR_Dataset2: Three exposure dataset for network 2 (001~100).

ref_#_aligned.tif : Low, midium, high exposure images for static scenes.
ref_exp.txt: Exposure compensation
ref_hdr_aligned_linear.hdr : Ground truth HDR image file. 
ldr_visualize.png: visualization of GT
Input_#_aligned.tif : Low, midium, high exposure images for Dynimic scenes.
input_exp.txt : Exposure compensation
